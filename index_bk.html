Zhiyang Fang is now a instructor at Sichuan University. He earned his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Sichuan University. He is currently involved in research work on information security. His research interests include reinforcement learning, deep learning, software security and software engineering etc. He has published over 10 research papers on international conferences and journals.

Notices:

Prospective Ph.D. students: I am looking for outstanding and highly motivated Ph.D. students to work together on deep reinforcement learning, information security and various software engineering topics. Please email me with your CV and transcripts.
